Model Prediction
cleavage assays with DNA competitors varied lengths and positions of complementarity predicted patterns of cleavage inhibition
Results
Inhibition of cleavage increased with increasing extent of complementarity at 3' end Inhibition increased with 12 or more bp complimentary Competitors with 3' end mismatch adjacent to PAM inert to binding
Results(Contd.)
Mismatch within first 2 nucleotides, Cas9-RNA looses interrogation ability Sequence homology adjacent to PAM is necessary for unwinding DNA competitor containing mismatches within itself and with crRNA also resulted in perfect binding
